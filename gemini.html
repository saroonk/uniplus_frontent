Program Overview

About the Scholarship

This scholarship is designed to support the **spouse or legal guardian** who accompanies a primary scholarship student abroad. It encourages companions to pursue higher education, provides family stability, and strengthens the Kingdom's competitiveness by investing in human capital growth.

Eligible companions may study a full-time **Bachelor’s, Master’s, PhD, or equivalent academic program** at accredited universities located in the same country as the primary student's scholarship.

Entry Requirements

Previous Qualification

The companion must have obtained the **previous academic level qualification** or a recognized equivalent. If the certificate was issued outside the Kingdom, **equivalency** is required.

No Prior Awards

The companion must **not** have already been awarded a scholarship or completed a qualification at the **same academic level**.

Primary Scholarship Recipient Status
  • **Must be on a scholarship** to study an academic program (Bachelor’s, Master’s, PhD, Fellowship).
  • **Must be actively studying** and not in postponement status.
  • Must have a scholarship decision issued as a **primary student, not as a companion**.
Residency

The companion must **reside together** with the primary scholarship recipient in the **same country and location** of the university.

Specific Conditions

Remaining Mission Period

At least **6 months** must remain in the primary scholarship's mission period at the start of study.

No Duplicate Companions

The primary scholarship student must not have previously added another companion at the same academic level.

Unconditional Admission

The companion must secure unconditional academic admission meeting the following criteria:

In a specialization within the approved tracks of the Scholarship Program.
At a university recognized by the Ministry of Education.
Automatic Termination

The companion’s scholarship **automatically ends** when the primary scholarship recipient’s mission is completed or terminated.
