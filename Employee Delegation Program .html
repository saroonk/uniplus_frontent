Employee Delegation Program (External) – General sector

Saudi Arabia • Vision 2030 • International Study

The Employee Delegation Program (External) supports Saudi government employees by enabling them to pursue higher academic qualifications abroad at accredited international institutions.

This program aims to enhance professional competencies, develop advanced skills, and prepare government talent for leadership roles that contribute to national development and the strategic objectives of Saudi Vision 2030.

Through this initiative, employees receive full scholarship support while studying in specialized fields that directly strengthen their governmental sector and improve institutional performance.

Program Objectives:
  • Strengthen the capabilities and skillsets of Saudi government personnel.
  • Equip future leaders with global educational exposure.
  • Support national workforce development through specialty-driven learning.
  • Align public sector roles with strategic and technological advancements.

Entry Requirements

To qualify for the scholarship, applicants must meet the following criteria:
  • Employer Nomination & Approval
    • Must obtain formal approval from their government organization (Ministry, Public Institution, or Government Agency).
    • A fully authorized study leave covering the scholarship duration is required.
  • Service Duration
    • Applicants usually must have completed at least 2 years of government service in a recognized position.
  • Professional Performance
    • Latest performance evaluation should be “Very Good” or higher.
  • Field of Study Relevance
    • Specialization Must Be Aligned With
      • The employee’s current role
      • A future career role defined by the employing authority
