Path of Excellence Scholarship
Supporting Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030

The Path of Excellence Scholarship is a premier national initiative by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to empower talented students with world-class education. By sponsoring studies at top international universities, this program strengthens the nation’s capabilities, advances economic development, and prepares future leaders in priority fields.

Program Objectives

  • Advance scientific and professional qualifications among Saudi youth
  • Build a competitive and innovative economy aligned with global standards
  • Support strategic and emerging sectors such as tourism, hospitality, business, and human resources
  • Enhance quality of life through fields like sports management and environmental sciences
  • Cultivate ambitious citizens who initiate growth and create meaningful opportunities for the future of the Kingdom

Required Majors & Specializations

  • Business Administration
  • Sports & Sports Management
  • Hospitality Management
  • Tourism & Tourism Management
  • Public Policy & Administration
  • Criminal Justice
  • Media & Communication
  • Social Sciences
  • Economics
  • Maritime Sciences
  • National Security
  • Environmental Sciences & Environmental Management
  • Anthropology
  • Political Science & International Relations
  • History
  • Philosophy
  • Geography
  • Human Resources
  • Social Service

Eligibility Requirements

  • Be a Saudi national
  • Maintain good conduct and disciplinary standing
  • Obtain unconditional direct admission to an approved Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD program
  • Enroll in a full-time academic or professional program (not executive-style programs)
  • Study in the main campus country of the chosen institution
  • Ensure consistent academic attendance
  • Provide qualification equivalency if the previous degree was earned outside KSA
  • Submit accurate documentation — any inconsistency may result in exclusion

Why Choose the Path of Excellence?

  • Supports top-priority career sectors under Vision 2030
  • Offers a wide range of modern and emerging fields
  • Enhances Saudi competitiveness in the global workforce
  • Develops leaders ready to elevate the Kingdom’s future

Building the Nation — One Scholar at a Time
The Path of Excellence Scholarship invests in the minds and skills of Saudi students today to secure a prosperous and ambitious Saudi Arabia tomorrow.

Emdad Scholarship (Master's Track) - Excellence Path – General Graduate Track (Master's)

The Emdad Scholarship is a core track within the Saudi "Path of Excellence" scholarship program, designed to elevate the specialized skills and professional qualifications of Saudi students through Master’s level study at highly-ranked international institutions.

This pathway is a strategic investment aimed at directly addressing specific, high-demand labor market needs and fostering advanced expertise essential for the successful realization of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030.

By guiding scholars to pursue critical specializations at top universities, the program ensures the Kingdom gains highly skilled professionals ready to drive innovation and lead key national sectors.

Entry Requirements

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Must hold Saudi nationality
  • Must have obtained an unconditional admission offer to a qualifying Master’s program
  • Must typically hold a minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 (or equivalent)
  • The university must meet the ranking criteria set by the scholarship authorities
Additional Conditions for the General Graduate Track (Master’s):
  • The university must be ranked within the program’s approved top 200 list
  • Admission must be final and unconditional, issued by the university
  • If studying in a non-English speaking country, conditional admission may be permitted (with language preparation included)
