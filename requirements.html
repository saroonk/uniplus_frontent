Entry requirements

Find out more about the general entry requirements for graduate taught and graduate research degrees.

Master’s programmes (except MRes), Postgraduate and Q Certichdulate Diplomas

Applicants should hold first or upper second class Bachelor's degree from an appropriate UK university or an overseas qualification of an equivalent standard recognised by the education institution.

Candidates must sometimes enter with a first class Bachelor’s degree in a UK university or a qualification of an equivalent standard recognised by the education institution to obtain a place on specific programmes.

MRes programmes

Applicants should hold a first class Bachelor’s degree in a UK university or a qualification of an equivalent standard recognised by the education institution.

A first or upper second class Bachelor’s degree in a UK university or a qualification of an equivalent standard recognised by the education institution is sufficient for some of the MRes programmes.

For some candidates a pre-degree of a Master’s degree or an equivalent qualification is required in the specific area. Applicants must hold an equivalent qualification to a Master’s degree.

MPhil programmes

Applicants usually enter with a Master’s degree from a UK university. However, first class or upper second class Bachelor’s degrees or equivalent are sufficient for some programmes equivalent to MPhil, MRes or other Master’s degrees.

Graduate Diplomas/Graduate Certificates

For details on entry taught Graduate Diplomas or Graduate Certificates, please consult the prospectus.

MD(Res) programmes

Applicants should hold a medical degree and be registered with the GMC in the UK or an overseas medical qualification equivalent.
